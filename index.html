My adventures

Thailand

Phi-Phi island

I always wanted to visit Thailand! And so, my dream came true. My brother, his wife and I went on a trip in the summer of 2017. The beauty and culture of this country will forever remain in my heart.

It was an amazing journey! We traveled almost all the coast of Phuket and visited the island of Phi Phi, visited the island of monkeys and got into a storm, riding on elephants, visited all the points of view, it was indescribably amazing.

France

Paris

I wanted to meet in Paris my birthday. I wanted to drink a cup of coffee and a croissant near the Eiffel tower in the morning. And I did it!

Paris is a very charming city, with beautiful busy streets and lots of places of interest. I visited the Louvre and saw the picture of Mona Lisa, climbed to the top of the Eiffel tower, visited the Museum of modern art, drank fine French wine and enjoyed the company of myself in this town. Paris is so beautiful!

Turkey

Alanya

It was a spontaneous journey. My friends and I decided that we will go just two days before departure. All I've heard about Turkey from my friends is staying in a hotel. But in fact, it is a lot of interesting things.

We enjoyed the sea, warm weather, delicious food, went to the mountains, visited the medieval castle, perfectly preserved to our time, went to the huge reservoir, which supplies clean water to several cities, walked on the orange plantation and tried all the delights of duty free.
